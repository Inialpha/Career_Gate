About the project

This is a portfolio project in Holberton school. As a team project we decided to build this application to help job seekers get the necessary assistance in the areas of writing an outstanding resume and getting interview experience and insights from experts. We decided to build this project because of the passion we both have for the easy integration of fresh graduates into the job market. Fresh graduates always face the challenge of inexperience in their industry of interest as well as inexperience in the job seeking process. It is therefore our goal to solve the problem posed by the inexperience in the job seeking process by providing the needed experience.
